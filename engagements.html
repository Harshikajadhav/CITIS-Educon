Engagements

University Progression

The NEP 2020 focuses on quality higher education and it emphasises on all round development of every individual. The main aim is to enable individual learners to study deep into a specialized domain of their interest and develop 21st century capabilities across multiple disciplines such as science, technology, humanities, languages along side professional, vocational and technical subjects.


Vocational Education

Vocational Education and Training prepares students for jobs that are based on a specific trade or occupation. This technical or technology-based training develops expertise in a learner and makes the student employment ready. Vocational education courses are in accordance with the industry needs and demands and students today are pursuing careers in many industry domains through vocational courses.


Center of Excellence

CITIS offers a structured approach to Institutions, Colleges and Universities to setup COE (Centre Of Excellence) in collaboration with Corporates globally. The COE offers educational and industrial leadership, best of the academic practices, research orientation, technology and practical dimension to education, TTT (Train the trainer) programs and establishes a strong Academia-Industry collaboration.
