Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS)



Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) is an industry-recognized certification program that validates skills in Microsoft Office applications. With over 3.9 million jobs every year requesting Microsoft Office skills (in the United States alone), those who take and pass MOS certifications are empowered to stand out in the competitive job market, while simplifying daily tasks and fostering career growth.

MOS is the only Microsoft-endorsed certification program that validates Microsoft Office skills. It offers three certification versions, including Microsoft Office Specialist – Microsoft 365 Apps, Microsoft Office Specialist – Microsoft Office 2019, and Microsoft Office Specialist – Microsoft Office 2016.



The impact of Microsoft Office Specialist certification

MOS certification gives individuals the ability to achieve professional success in diverse professional environments, including business and finance, healthcare, computer science and IT, engineering, and more. It also empowers educators, employers, and the overall job industry.

  • For Students:
    It boosts academic performance, prepares them for the demands of higher education, and opens doors to internship and career opportunities.
  • For Educators:
    It provides a more engaged student body, increases their effectiveness, and supports meeting academic standards.
  • For Administrators:
    It lowers the number of dropouts and disciplinary cases, increases diplomas and degrees, as well as supports higher GPAs.
  • For Employers:
    It ensures a knowledgeable workforce, promotes quality and consistency, and demonstrates a commitment to excellence, ultimately driving competitiveness, customer satisfaction, and business success.

Become a Microsoft Office Specialist Associate or Expert


Leverage the power of combining certifications to demonstrate advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office with the MOS Associate or Expert certifications.

Students earn a certification for each MOS exam they pass, but if a student earns any three of the four available associate certifications, they will become a Microsoft Office Specialist – Associate and earn an additional certification for their accomplishment.

They can advance even further once they earn their Associate certification and earn two of the Microsoft Office Specialist – Expert exams and achieve the highest certification within the MOS program.
