University Progression



The NEP 2020 focuses on quality higher education and it emphasises on all round development of every individual. The main aim is to enable individual learners to study deep into a specialized domain of their interest and develop 21st century capabilities across multiple disciplines such as science, technology, humanities, languages along side professional, vocational and technical subjects.



University Progression Model

We offer some of the finest engagement programs to universities and colleges in India. We are suggesting the different ways to collaborate through Certification Courses, VAP (Value Added Programs) for the engineering students at the university.
  • For Credit Integrated Learning: CITIS programs can be integrated into the curriculum of the College/University. With the new education policy CITIS can provide some of our courses against credits. CITIS will be teaching these courses and University can then conduct proctored examination post completion of such courses prior to allotment of credits to students. Joint certification can be issued for students.
  • Honors Program: University can offer honors program to the regular B.Tech/B.E./MBA/BBA/PGDM students which will over and above the conventional programs by offering CITIS courses. E.g. AI and ML, Cyber Security, Dev Ops, Digital Marketing etc. These programs can be offered for additional credits thereby students getting an option to get specialized degrees.
  • Diploma Program: A fully online Diploma or Post Graduate Diploma programs can be started by the college which could be powered by CITIS. These programs could be open for working professionals, students, professionals who are looking for diploma course from College/University.
  • Upskilling Program: The selective courses from CITIS would be made available for students in the current digital skills required by the industry. These could be both for credit or otherwise thereby providing more multidisciplinary learning options to the students of College/University. We have created stack of such programs commencing from Semester IV to Semester VII.
