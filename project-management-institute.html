PMI Project Management Ready™ is a one-semester certification program for teachers pioneering project management-specific courses or instructors teaching other career and tech ed classes who want to equip students with foundational skills in project management. With this certification, students will be prepared for entry-level jobs such as project coordinator, event planner, and project team member and be on the pathway to further certification from the Project Management Institute® (PMI).

Integrating the PMI Project Management Ready learning resources and certification into the classroom helps students gain a credential in project management and prepare them for their next step in education or a career.

PMI is the world’s leading association for those who consider project, program or portfolio management their profession. Through global advocacy, collaboration, education and research, they work to prepare more than three million professionals around the world for The Project Economy: the coming economy in which work and individuals are organized around projects. Candidates for this certification will be expected to have key conceptual knowledge and principles of project management through at least 150 hours of instruction.

The PMI Project Management Ready™ certification is designed to test and validate foundational concepts and knowledge in project management. This is an entry-level certification that does not require any prior work experience in project management and can help candidates take their first step towards preparing to become a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)®.



What are the benefits of certifying?

  • Everyone completes projects and can benefit from project management skills. This new certification allows educators to engage with students interested in project management.
  • Earning a professional credential gives students a competitive edge in the job market as it demonstrates their knowledge and competencies.
  • Earning a credential prior to graduation provides students a head start as they pursue additional education, internship opportunities, or the military.

PMI Project Management Ready fills the need for an industry-recognized certification that allows career and technical education institutions to add value to their programs.
